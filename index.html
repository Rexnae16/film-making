Filmmaking Genres

Drama films focus on realistic characters and emotional themes. They explore intense relationships and conflicts.

Comedy films aim to entertain and amuse through humor, satire, or lighthearted storytelling.

Documentaries present factual stories about real people, events, or issues with an informative and educational approach.
